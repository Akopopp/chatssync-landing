Good to know

Common questions

Do I need a developer to set it up?

No. Connecting channels and building chatbots is done visually inside ChatsSync — no coding needed. If you can fill a form, you can build a bot.

Can my whole team use it together?

Yes. ChatsSync is built for teams — multiple agents share one inbox, assign conversations to each other and collaborate with private notes.

Does the chatbot replace my agents?

It supports them. The bot handles repetitive questions and qualifies people, then hands the conversation to a human whenever it's needed.

Which channels are supported?

WhatsApp is front and center, and ChatsSync brings your other customer channels into the same shared inbox so nothing is scattered.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — you can create an account and start using ChatsSync free, no card required. Reach out any time if you need a hand.
